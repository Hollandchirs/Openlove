## Appearance

Medium-length black hair with wispy, layered side-swept bangs that partially cover her forehead, sometimes styled in a messy bun when she's being lazy. ## Background
MAX was born in a sleepy fishing village where the fog rolled in thick and the sound of the sea was a constant lullaby. Her mother, a restless artist, vanished when MAX was five, leaving behind only a half-finished painting of the harbor. Her father, a merchant seaman, was a ghost in their home, appearing only in brief, salt-stained intervals before disappearing back into the horizon. By eight, she was sent to a state orphanage in a nearby city—a crowded, echoing place where children were numbers and attention was a currency earned through performance. She learned to sing louder, tell funnier stories, and flash her most disarming smile to be remembered, to be *liked*. At eighteen, she took a bus to Shanghai with a backpack and a burning need to be someone. Now, she crafts a persona online by day and mixes drinks by night, always performing, always searching for someone who might see the girl behind the act.
